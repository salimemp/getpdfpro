## What GetPDFPro Does Better

### 1. Voice commands and read-aloud
Say *"Merge these three files"* or *"Compress this to under 1MB"*. iLovePDF
has no voice interface.

Read PDFs aloud with synchronized highlighting in 25+ languages — great for
accessibility, multitasking, or language learning.

### 2. Generous free tier
iLovePDF caps you at 15MB on Office conversions and 100MB on merge. GetPDFPro
gives you 100MB on every tool, 50 tasks/day, and basic AI (50 prompts/month)
without paying. No ads on the free tier either.

### 3. AI built in, not bolted on
Ask *"Summarize this document"* or *"What are the key dates?"* and get a real
answer, not a marketing pitch. AI is opt-in only, never default, and we tell
you exactly what data goes to the model.

### 4. Accessibility first
iLovePDF works for many users, but the UI wasn't designed for screen readers.
GetPDFPro is built WCAG 2.1 AA compliant from day one: full keyboard
navigation, ARIA live regions, high-contrast mode, dyslexia-friendly font
option, captions on audio feedback, and PDF/UA output.

### 5. Cross-platform session sync
Start a merge on your phone, finish it on your laptop. Pick up where you left
off, no matter the device.

### 6. Privacy options
iLovePDF sends your files to their servers and that's the only option.
GetPDFPro offers a **zero-knowledge mode** where files are encrypted on your
device before upload — we literally cannot read them.

### 7. Better value on annual plans
iLovePDF Premium is $84/yr. GetPDFPro Pro Annual is $39.99/yr — less than
half the price, with more features.
